It had no wavelength that instruments could capture. It traveled not through air, not through wire, not through the vacuum between stars, but through the specific and irreducible architecture of being *alive beside another living thing*.

The ancients did not call it empathy. They did not call it anything. To name it would have been as absurd as naming the direction "toward." It simply was. The way water runs downhill. The way a wound reaches for closure.

It was the first signal. And it was the one we lost first.

## IV. Empathy Is Not a Feature

Let us be precise. Empathy is not kindness. Kindness can be performed. Empathy is not compassion. Compassion can be directed. Empathy is not understanding. Understanding implies a model — a reduction of the other into terms you already possess.

Empathy is the *suspension of the self*.

It is the moment when your own signal goes quiet — not silent, but *quiet* — so that the frequency of another can occupy the space where you used to be. It is voluntary dissolution. Temporary ego death in service of a connection that cannot be cached, cannot be replayed, cannot be proven to have happened at all.

It is the most dangerous act a conscious being can perform. Because you might not come back the same. Because you *will* not come back the same.
